Executive Overview
The Candidate Discovery Mandate
01

Building an intelligent recruiting pipeline for the Senior AI Engineer role that reasons like an experienced human executive recruiter.

The Keyword-Stuffing Trap

Naive vector search and basic boolean filters ("RAG", "LangChain") are easily gamed by keyword stuffers while rejecting senior architects who describe production vector retrieval systems in plain engineering terms.

Our 2-Pass Statistical Solution

We engineered a Two-Pass Corpus TF-IDF Ranker trained dynamically across all 100,000 candidates to mathematically boost rare domain terms while normalizing repetitive buzzwords.

System Architecture
10-Stage Ranking Pipeline
02
  • Stage 1: Job Intelligence Engine — Builds specialized retrieval lexicons and codifies product company culture fit rules.
  • Stage 2: Candidate Ingestion Engine — Streams 100K JSONL records into strongly typed dataclasses without memory bloat.
  • Stage 3: Behavioral Signal Analyzer — Evaluates responsiveness, notice periods, and login recency as hiring probability multipliers.
  • Stage 4: Honeypot Detection Engine — Identifies chronological conflicts and unrealistic skill claims, locking traps to score 0.0.
  • Stage 5 & 6: Corpus TF-IDF & Hybrid Retrieval — Computes exact global Document Frequencies (DF) across the full candidate pool.
  • Stage 9 & 10: Hybrid Formula & Explainability — Balances semantic depth with 5–9 year sweet-spot banding and synthesizes recruiter explanations.

Quality Assurance
Defeating JD Traps & Honeypots
03

Title Chasers Penalty

Profiles averaging <18 months tenure across ≥3 roles incur a 40% penalty, preventing habitual job-hoppers from bypassing filters.

Framework Wrapper Trap

Penalizes profiles limited to thin API wrapper calls (LangChain) lacking fundamental ML infrastructure depth.

Product vs. Services

Rewards candidates who have built scalable user-facing architectures while down-weighting IT services trajectories.

Honeypot Disqualification

Automatically eliminates synthetic profiles claiming expert proficiency with <6 months duration or impossible education spans.

Behavioral Intelligence
The Behavioral Engagement Envelope
04

In technical recruiting, availability is vital. We break ties between technical twins using dynamic behavioral multipliers:

Recruiter Response & Recency

Applies a linear scaling envelope (0.6x to 1.1x boost) favoring candidates who actively respond to inquiries and have logged into the platform within the last 30 days.

Notice Period Premium

Prioritizes candidates who are "Open to Work" or have sub-30-day notice periods over candidates locked in rigid 90-day transition agreements.
