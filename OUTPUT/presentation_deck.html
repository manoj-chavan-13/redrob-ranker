Executive Summary
Engineering AI Discovery for Senior Mandates
01

Targeting the Founding Team Senior AI Engineer role requires an AI talent discovery pipeline that avoids superficial keyword matching and evaluates architectural depth, production scale, and hiring probability.

The Keyword Stuffing Vulnerability Conventional RAG

Standard vector similarity filters fail because junior candidates stuff resumes with buzzwords (LangChain, RAG, Pinecone), while exceptional senior architects who describe custom retrieval infrastructure in plain engineering text get filtered out.

Our Two-Pass Statistical Solution Antigravity AI

We implement a Statistical Corpus TF-IDF Ranker that first streams all 100,000 profiles to learn global document frequencies (DF). Rare terms (qlora, weaviate, ndcg) receive dynamically boosted semantic weights, normalizing generic noise.

System Architecture
10-Stage Candidate Ranking Pipeline
02
  • Stage 1: Job Intelligence Codifies specialized domain lexicons (RETRIEVAL_TERMS, VECTOR_DB_TERMS, EVAL_TERMS) and establishes culture fit weighting (prioritizing product companies over IT services firms).
  • Stage 2: Ingestion Engine High-speed JSONL streaming parser mapping raw records into strongly typed Python dataclasses (CandidateProfile) with zero memory bloat (<150 MB RAM).
  • Stage 3: Behavioral Signal Evaluates 23 platform signals as a hiring probability modifier ($0.6\text{x}$ to $1.1\text{x}$ envelope), boosting responsive candidates with immediate notice periods.
  • Stage 4: Honeypot Detection Rigorously screens chronological conflicts and synthetic skill claims (e.g., claiming expert skill with <6m tenure), assigning traps a hard score of 0.0.
  • Stage 5–8: Hybrid Scoring Combines statistical TF-IDF domain scores with experience sweet-spot banding (5–9 years target band) and anti-job-hopping penalties.
  • Stage 9–10: Explainability Synthesizes multi-dimensional scoring into human-readable 1–2 sentence recruiter narratives justifying candidate rank and confidence level.

Quality Assurance
Defeating JD Traps & Synthetic Honeypots
04

Title Chasers Penalty -40% Multiplier

Habitual job-hoppers inflate titles without building depth. If a candidate averages <18 months tenure across ≥3 roles, our scoring engine applies a sharp 0.60x penalty, preventing serial job-hoppers from taking top rank positions.

Framework Wrapper Filter Anti-Stuffer

Candidates listing superficial wrapper libraries (LangChain, basic OpenAI prompts) without foundational systems keywords receive a downgraded semantic relevance multiplier compared to core systems builders.

Product vs. Services Alignment Culture Fit

For a Founding Team role, end-to-end product ownership is essential. Candidates from pure product companies receive positive affinity weighting over candidates spending their careers at outsourced IT services firms.

Honeypot Disqualification Zero Tolerance

Synthetic trap candidates injected into the dataset (e.g., claiming 10+ years of PyTorch experience when graduated 2 years ago, or expert proficiency with <6m duration) are flagged and locked to a score of 0.0000.
