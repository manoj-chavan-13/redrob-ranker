🎯 Redrob AI Intelligent Candidate Discovery & Ranking System

Slide 1: Executive Summary & The Problem

  • The Problem: Conventional recruiting systems rely on superficial keyword matching ("RAG", "LangChain", "Pinecone"). This creates severe vulnerability to keyword stuffers and overlooks high-caliber engineers who describe complex architectures in plain language.
  • Our Solution: A Two-Pass Statistical Corpus TF-IDF Ranker that streams and trains dynamically across the complete 100,000-candidate pool.
  • Key Outcome: Rare domain expertise is mathematically rewarded, buzzwords get normalized, and candidate availability is modeled as an essential hiring probability multiplier.
Offline CPU Execution Time: ~24 Seconds | Memory Consumption: ~145 MB

🏗️ Slide 2: 10-Stage Pipeline Architecture

  • Stage 1: Job Intelligence Engine — Constructs domain lexicons and codifies culture fit rules (prioritizing product companies over services firms).
  • Stage 2: Candidate Intelligence Engine — Streams JSONL records into strongly typed Python models without loading unnecessary objects.
  • Stage 3: Behavioral Signal Analyzer — Models signals as hiring probability modifiers (Availability, Responsiveness, Market Demand, Trust).
  • Stage 4: Honeypot Detection Engine — Scans for chronological anomalies and synthetic skill claims, locking honeypots to score 0.0.
  • Stage 5 & 6: Corpus TF-IDF & Hybrid Retrieval — Learns exact global Document Frequencies (DF) and Inverse Document Frequency (IDF) weights across all 100,000 records.
  • Stage 7 & 9: Hybrid Ranking Engine — Combines normalized semantic depth, experience sweet-spot banding (5–9 years), anti-job-hopping penalties, and behavioral multipliers into deterministic final ranks.
  • Stage 10: Explainability Engine — Synthesizes technical depth and engagement signals into human-readable 1–2 sentence recruiter narratives.

🛡️ Slide 3: Defeating JD Traps & Honeypots

  • Title Chasers Penalty: Down-weights profiles averaging <18 months tenure across ≥3 roles by 40%, preventing chronic job-hoppers from gaming seniority filters.
  • Framework Enthusiasts Trap: Penalizes candidates with only thin wrapper experience (LangChain/OpenAI calls) lacking foundational ML engineering depth.
  • Consulting vs. Product Focus: Recognizes candidates who have shipped end-to-end user-facing product architectures while penalizing pure services/consulting trajectories.
  • Honeypot Elimination: Rigorous validation checks automatically disqualify synthetic profiles claiming "Expert" proficiency with <6 months duration or impossible education spans.

📈 Slide 4: Behavioral Engagement Envelope

In real-world recruiting, candidate availability is as critical as technical capability. We break ties between "behavioral twins" using dynamic multipliers:

  • Recruiter Response Rate: Linear scaling favoring responsive candidates (0.6x to 1.1x boost).
  • Recency Penalty: Discounts profiles inactive for >3 or >6 months.
  • Notice Period Premium: Boosts candidates with immediate or sub-30-day notice periods.
  • Trust & Reliability: Accounts for interview completion rates and verified profile attributes.
