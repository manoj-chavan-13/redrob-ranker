System Architecture
10-Stage Candidate Ranking Pipeline
02
  • Stage 1: Job Intelligence Engine — Builds curated domain knowledge taxonomies (Vector DBs, Retrieval, Quant Eval) and establishes culture fit weighting favoring product builders.
  • Stage 2: High-Speed Ingestion — Streams 100,000 raw JSONL records into strongly typed Python dataclasses (CandidateProfile) with zero memory bloat (peak RAM under 150 MB).
  • Stage 3: Behavioral Signal Analyzer — Evaluates 23 platform activity signals as hiring probability modifiers (0.60x to 1.10x envelope), prioritizing responsive profiles.
  • Stage 4: Honeypot Detection Engine — Detects chronological anomalies and synthetic claims (e.g., expert skill with under 6 months tenure), locking traps to score 0.0.
  • Stage 5–8: Hybrid Scoring Formula — Combines normalized TF-IDF semantic depth with experience sweet-spot banding (5–9 years target band) and anti-job-hopping penalties.
  • Stage 9–10: Explainability Generator — Synthesizes multi-dimensional scoring into human-readable 1–2 sentence recruiter narratives justifying exact candidate placement.

Quality Assurance
Defeating JD Traps & Synthetic Profiles
04

Title Chasers Penalty -40% Penalty

Habitual job-hoppers inflate titles without building depth. If a candidate averages under 18 months tenure across 3 or more roles, our engine applies a sharp 0.60x score multiplier.

Framework Wrapper Filter Anti-Stuffer

Profiles listing superficial wrapper libraries (LangChain, basic API prompts) without core systems keywords receive downgraded semantic multipliers compared to infrastructure builders.

Product vs. Services Alignment Culture Fit

For a Founding Team role, end-to-end product ownership is essential. Candidates from pure product software companies receive positive affinity weighting over IT services firms.

Honeypot Elimination Zero Tolerance

Synthetic trap candidates injected into the dataset (e.g., claiming 10+ years of PyTorch experience when graduated 2 years ago) are flagged and locked to a score of 0.0000.
